# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-26

## What we collect

saas-init is a CLI tool that runs locally on your machine. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking.

## Third-party services

When you configure your generated project you will provide API keys for services you choose (Clerk, Stripe, Supabase, etc.). Those keys are written to `.env.local` on your local machine only. saas-init never sees or stores them.
